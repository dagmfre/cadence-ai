Why this card is shaped this way

The payload is the content. A summary (“comment on #12”) is not enough to consent to; the reviewer needs the words that will appear under their name. So the quoted block gets the space, wraps rather than truncates, and sits on the deepest surface so it reads as the literal thing. Approve is the only filled button on the row; Dismiss stays ghost.
